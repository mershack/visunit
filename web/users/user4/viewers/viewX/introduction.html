How to interpret the tree visualization for the tasks

Please take some time to review the following information on how to interpret tree visualization.

You will perform the task with either a radial tree hierarchy or a vertical tree hierarchy as shown in the images below


A tree has a parent-child hierarchy, with the root being the highest on the hierarchy, and the leaves being the lowest on the hierarchy. In the radial tree, the root is the node at the center of the circle, and in the vertical tree, the root is the top-most node. For example in the above visualization, the root node is "flare".
