Adjacency Matrices

This is an 'adjacency matrix'. It is a way to show connectivity (links) of entities (nodes). Nodes are shown horizontally and vertically and if there is a dot in the matrix at their intersecting columns and rows, then they are connected. The visualization is interactive: you can hover over nodes to turn them and their links green; and you can click on nodes to select them (turn red). You can select multiple nodes, and you can deselect a node by clicking on it again.
Two of the tasks will require you to turn nodes blue (the 'neighbor' task and the 'path' task). You can do so by holding CTRL pressed while you click on nodes. See the examples below.
